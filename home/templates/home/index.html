🌿 Welcome to Wild Wellness Escape 🌿

Looking for a getaway that's part luxury retreat, part wild adventure, and 100% unforgettable? You've found it!


Unplug. Slow down. Breathe deep.


Discover the power of the great outdoors to heal your mind, body, and spirit. Away in the heart of untouched wilderness, Wild Wellness Escape invites you to spend an unforgettable time surrounded by nature's magic. Wake up to the gentle gaze of a curious deer outside your window or the playful rustle of rabbits and foxes.

Whether you're craving mindful meditation, thrilling outdoor adventures, or simply a peaceful spot to recharge, our Wild Wellness experience has something just for you. 👉 Psst... see our little squirrel friend? Give it a click and let the adventure begin! 🐿️

It’s a wild adventure for the body, mind, and spirit, but make it luxurious:

  • Reconnect with nature.
  • Make your own fire and cook dinner under the trees.
  • Harvest wild garlic, make your own fresh pesto.
  • Brew your tea from wild flowers you gathered yourself.
  • Learn about the healing power of plants and make your own cosmetics.
  • Relax in the sauna and take a dip in the lake.
  • Enjoy a delicious meal made from local, organic ingredients.
  • Dive into wild waters. Paddle into the horizon.
  • Catch your dinner from the lake and grill it while the sun sets.
  • Trek through untamed trails and climb until the world opens up before you.
  • Breathe deep, stretch wide, and let the mountain air fill your lungs with freedom.

When you're done living your best wild life, you get to cozy up in a gorgeous cabin, snuggle under soft blankets, and glass of wine by the fire.

Stay wild. Stay cozy. Simple pleasures, wild beauty, and the freedom.


So what are you waiting for? Grab your boots, your swimsuit, and your appetite. The wild is calling and your cabin is waiting....
